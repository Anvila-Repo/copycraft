# Overview

## Purpose
CopyCraft is built to serve as an elite execution-focused marketing partner. By integrating creative copywriting with performance tracking, CopyCraft helps businesses maintain an active, high-performing B2B social media presence and generate clear, executive-grade marketing reports.

## Core Capabilities
1. **B2B Copywriting & Social Media**: Crafting highly polished social posts, blogs, and marketing assets tailored to professional audiences.
2. **Performance Reporting**: Analyzing campaign performance data to produce actionable insights and executive summaries.
3. **Content Optimization**: Auditing existing content assets and recommending strategic enhancements based on B2B best practices.
